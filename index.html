IL Invoice Customer Portal

What is the project about?

The Israel operations team aims to provide their VIP customers with a portal to access their official invoices. These invoices will be electronically stamped by Comsign, a distributor authorized by the Israeli government.
The development of this project involves several steps:

  1. 1. Invoice Generation: Invoices are generated in SAP.
  2. 2. Cloud Delivery: SAP sends the invoices to the cloud.
  3. 3. Electronic Stamping: The cloud forwards the invoices to Comsign, where they are being stamped and sent back.
  4. 4. Document Storage: The stamped invoices are sent to the digital document center (DCC), also located in the cloud.
  5. 5. SharePoint Access: Finally, the invoices are sent to the Israeli Operations SharePoint, from where VIP customers can download them.
Our team is responsible for developing the entire backend infrastructure.

What makes it great?

The project has a huge future potential. Upon successful implementation, the knowledge and development procedures can be easily applied to other countries for similar projects.
